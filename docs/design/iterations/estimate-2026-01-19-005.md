**Secondary Effects** (other metrics):
- regularity: Expected +5% to +10% improvement

- density: Expected unchanged (syncopation width doesn't affect hit counts directly)

- voiceSeparation: Expected unchanged (voice positions unchanged)

- Mitigation:
